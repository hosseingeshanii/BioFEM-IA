Detailed Description

Persistent DMPlex geometry context stored in FE.

Built once by FEM_DMPlexGeomSetup() during initialization. Holds the distributed DM, per-rank stencil layout, coordinate-exchange SF, and the local-to-original vertex mapping needed for parallel coordinate updates.

Destroyed by FEM_DMPlexGeomDestroy().
